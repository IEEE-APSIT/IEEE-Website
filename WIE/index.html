About WIE

The goal of Women In Engineering is to bridge this glaring divide and encourage women to take up more leadership positions. We aim to create a more inclusive atmosphere, especially in the field of engineering. Webinars, seminars and various other interactive activities are organised regularly, apart from the conferences and magazines arranged by IEEE. These webinars and conferences also double as excellent platforms to forge new connections in the corporate world. With active members and a dedicated executive committee, we hope to change the face of engineering!
